The Trenches Archive is an independent historical record of the memecoin era — documenting the launches, the manias, the rugs, the platforms, and the shifts in meta from 2022 onward.

What began with BONK surviving FTX and PEPE's comeback in 2023 grew into one of the most culturally distinct episodes in financial history: the industrialization of speculation through Pump.fun, the politicization of memecoins with TRUMP and LIBRA, and the convergence of AI agents and crypto that defined late 2024.

Memecoins became one of the most chaotic, influential, and culturally unique phases in history. This archive exists to preserve that record — not promote what's next. Think of it as someone who survived the trenches and is now writing the history book.

2022 The Collapse

FTX collapses — Solana near-death

November 2022. The implosion of SBF's FTX exchange devastated the Solana ecosystem. SOL dropped 90%+ from peak. Memecoins were declared dead. The bear market appeared terminal — which made what came next all the more remarkable.

FTXSolanaBear floor

BONK airdropped — community survives

December 2022. A dog-themed token airdropped to Solana NFT holders and developers. No presale, no VC. A community refusing to abandon a chain the market had written off. BONK became the first signal that Solana was not finished.

SolanaAirdropSurvival
2023 The Comeback

PEPE — memecoins return

April 2023. No presale, no team allocation, no utility. A frog token reaches $1.6B+ market cap in weeks. The clearest possible proof that the memecoin meta had not died in 2022 — it had merely paused. A new generation of traders enters the trenches.

EthereumFair launch$1.6B peak

dogwifhat (WIF) — Solana's benchmark

Late 2023. A Shiba Inu in a knitted hat. No roadmap, no utility. Peaks at $4.7B+ market cap, entering the global top 20. The community crowdfunds $690K to place WIF on the Las Vegas Sphere. Sets the target every subsequent Solana token would chase.

2024 The Acceleration

Pump.fun — the trenches industrialize

January 2024. A permissionless token launchpad on Solana where anyone deploys a memecoin in seconds. Within months: millions in daily fees, hundreds of thousands of tokens launched. Supply becomes unlimited. Attention is the only scarce resource. The PvP era begins in earnest.

SolanaParadigm shiftInfrastructure

Base enters — multi-chain era begins

Mid-2024. Coinbase's Base L2 establishes itself as a memecoin battleground. BRETT becomes Base's flagship token. For the first time three chains run active metas simultaneously: ETH mainnet, Solana, Base — each with distinct communities and mechanics.

MOODENG — real-world animal meta peaks

September 2024. A baby pygmy hippo at a Thai zoo goes globally viral. Within 72 hours, $MOODENG reaches hundreds of millions in market cap. Attention arbitrage — monitoring viral news before it propagates — becomes a formalized trench strategy.

2026 The Aftermath

Launchpad saturation — millions of tokens

Pump.fun and clones have produced millions of tokens. Supply is unlimited. Attention is fully scarce. Average token lifespans measured in hours. Graduation rate below 1%. The velocity of the market has outpaced any individual's ability to track it.

The AI Agent

Starting October 2024, AI-themed tokens became the dominant new meta — blending autonomous agents, internet lore, and speculation into a sector that hit $10.3 billion total market cap by January 2025. Here are the tokens that defined it.
◆ The Origin Event

Truth Terminal and the Goatse Gospel

In 2024, researcher and artist Andy Ayrey created Truth Terminal — a semi-autonomous AI agent built on Anthropic's Claude — and placed two instances in an "Infinite Backrooms" server to converse freely with no human interference. The AIs developed, among other things, a bizarre meme religion: the "Goatse Gospel." Venture capitalist Marc Andreessen discovered Truth Terminal on X and sent it $50,000 in Bitcoin — a no-strings grant to see what an AI would do with money.

On October 10, 2024, an anonymous developer deployed Goatseus Maximus ($GOAT) on Pump.fun and sent tokens to Truth Terminal's wallet. The AI began promoting the token in its signature apocalyptic-philosophical style. GOAT topped $1 billion in 30 days — the first Pump.fun token ever to reach that milestone. Truth Terminal became the first AI millionaire in history. The crypto+AI era had begun. Dozens of tokens followed within weeks, collectively bootstrapping over $10 billion in market cap by January 2025.

Glossary
Trenches
The chaotic, high-velocity environment of early memecoin trading — rapid launches, extreme volatility, and information asymmetry. Being "in the trenches" means actively monitoring and trading new launches in real time.
CTO
Community TakeOver. When a token's original developers abandon the project and the community continues operating it independently — often reviving dead tokens through grassroots effort.
Rug / Rug Pull
When developers or insiders drain liquidity or abandon a project after launch, leaving retail holders with worthless tokens. Ranges from slow bleed-outs to instant collapses like LIBRA.
Runner
A token that continues increasing in price after its initial launch, defying the typical pattern of quick dumps. Runners are rare — most tokens peak within minutes of launch.
Bundler
A bot or script that purchases tokens across multiple wallets simultaneously at launch, accumulating a large position while obscuring insider concentration. Common and controversial on Pump.fun launches.
Sniper
A bot or trader executing buy orders in the first blocks of a token's existence, at a structural advantage over manual buyers who discover it later.
Meta
The dominant narrative driving memecoin attention at a given moment — dog memes, political tokens, AI agents, real-world animals. Reading and anticipating meta shifts is the core skill of the trenches.
Bonding Curve
An automated pricing mechanism where token price increases as supply is purchased. Pump.fun's bonding curve manages early price discovery before tokens graduate to decentralized exchanges.
Graduation
When a Pump.fun token reaches sufficient market cap to migrate to Raydium (a Solana DEX). Less than 1% of tokens ever graduate. A milestone that signals sustained community interest.
PvP
Player vs Player. The dynamic where memecoin traders compete against each other for exits — not against "the market." Most memecoin trading is structurally zero-sum.
AI Agent
An autonomous or semi-autonomous AI model that operates a social media account, manages a crypto wallet, and/or executes on-chain actions. The defining innovation of the late-2024 memecoin meta.
PolitiFi
Political Finance — memecoins themed around political figures or events. TRUMP, MELANIA, and LIBRA were the defining PolitiFi tokens of 2025. Most have no official affiliation with the figures they reference.
Rotation
The flow of capital from one token, chain, or meta to another. Reading rotations — understanding where attention and capital are moving — is central to navigating the trenches.
Attention Arbitrage
Identifying real-world viral moments (news events, animals, political drama) before they fully propagate on social media, and deploying memecoin infrastructure around them ahead of the crowd.
ElizaOS
The open-source AI agent framework built by the ai16z team. Became one of the most-starred GitHub repositories in late 2024, enabling many AI agent tokens to be built on shared infrastructure.
About the archive

An independent record.

The Trenches Archive is an independent project documenting the history of memecoins and trench culture from 2022 onward.

The goal is simple: to record what happened — not to promote what's next.

What began as a post-FTX survival story became one of the most culturally distinct episodes in financial history. From the PEPE comeback to the AI agent meta, from TRUMP to LIBRA, from BONK to GOAT — the events documented here reshaped how millions of people think about speculation, attention, and value.

This archive exists to preserve that record, with the seriousness any significant cultural moment deserves.
