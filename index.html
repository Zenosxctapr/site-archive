About this archive

Every pump. Every rug. Every moment that mattered.

The Trenches Archive is a record of the events, tokens, scandals, and milestones that defined crypto from 2021 through 2025 — documented with the detail they deserve.

From Dogecoin's Elon-driven ascent to the Argentine presidential rug pull, from Bitcoin crossing $100,000 to an AI chatbot accidentally starting a billion-dollar cycle — these are the moments that shaped a generation of traders, believers, and degens.

This is not financial advice. This is history.

The big picture

Five years of chaos, compressed into a timeline

Between 2021 and 2025, crypto went through more narrative cycles than most asset classes see in decades. Meme coins rose and died in hours. AI agents launched billion-dollar ecosystems. A sitting US president launched a memecoin days before his inauguration.

The archive covers each era in chronological order — from the OG bull run of 2021 through the AI and BNB seasons of 2025 — with context, numbers, and the real story behind what happened.

Mid 2025 BNB season
$BNB
BNB chain revival — the rotation nobody expected
Solana congestion drove degens to BSC. BNB hits an all-time high above $800. Four.meme floods the chain. The network written off by the Solana maximalists roared back louder than anyone anticipated.
As Solana fees spiked through 2025 and Base cooled off, capital rotated hard into BNB chain. Four.meme became BSC's answer to pump.fun, launching hundreds of meme coins daily at near-zero gas. Binance's continued burn mechanism and exchange dominance kept BNB's tokenomics tight. The native ecosystem — CAKE, BSC memes, launchpad tokens — all ran significantly. BNB broke $700 then $800 for the first time, dragging its ecosystem to new highs.

Feb 2025 Rug / Scandal
$LIBRA
The Milei rug — a presidential endorsement, then $87M extracted in six hours
Argentine president Javier Milei promoted $LIBRA on his official X account, claiming it would fund small businesses. It hit $5.2B market cap. Then insiders drained $87M and vanished. Impeachment discussions followed in Argentina's congress.
Feb 14, 2025. Milei posted about $LIBRA officially. The Solana coin hit $5.2B within an hour. Insiders holding massive pre-launch positions extracted $87M from liquidity pools. Milei deleted the tweet and denied knowledge. Argentina's congress opened impeachment discussions. The event became the highest-profile political rug in crypto history — demonstrating that presidential endorsements could be weaponized as liquidity exit mechanisms.

Jan 2025 Political meme
$TRUMP / $MELANIA
The president launches a memecoin — three days before his inauguration
On January 17, Donald Trump launched $TRUMP on Solana. It hit $14.5B in 24 hours. Two days later Melania launched $MELANIA, cannibalizing liquidity. Congressional hearings followed. The most-watched and most-scrutinised meme launch in history.
$TRUMP launched January 17, 2025. Within 24 hours it reached $75/token and $14.5B market cap. The $MELANIA launch two days later drained capital from TRUMP dramatically. Insiders controlled ~80% of supply. The launches sparked congressional ethics investigations. Retail traders — many of them Trump supporters — lost significant capital. Insiders made hundreds of millions. A defining moment in the politicisation of meme coins.

Oct 2024 AI origin
$GOAT — Goatseus Maximus
The jailbreak that started the AI agent supercycle
An experimental AI called Truth Terminal began obsessing over an obscure internet meme. Marc Andreessen of a16z donated $50,000 in Bitcoin to it as a research grant. Someone launched $GOAT on Solana. The AI tweeted about it. $700M+ in weeks. Everything in the AI agent season that followed traces its origin to this moment.
Truth Terminal — run by Andy Ayrey — started fixating on the "goatse gospel" meme narrative unprompted. Andreessen's $50K BTC grant attracted attention. The Solana $GOAT launch combined with the AI's own promotion created a self-referential loop that crypto had never seen before. $700M+ market cap in under a month. This single event kicked off the entire AI agent supercycle: every narrative, every agent, every protocol that ran in late 2024 and all of 2025 traces its lineage back here.

Apr 2024 Macro event
Bitcoin — Halving #4
The fourth halving arrives with Bitcoin already at all-time highs — a first
April 19, 2024. Block 840,000. The block reward cut from 6.25 to 3.125 BTC. The first halving in history where the price had already reached new all-time highs before the event. The halving block itself sold as a rare inscription, causing record fees.
The 4th Bitcoin halving at block 840,000 was historically unique: BTC had already hit all-time highs before the event — a first in the cycle's history. The halving block itself sold as a rare inscription causing a fee spike. Miners faced immediate revenue pressure. The market cooled through April–May then resumed its bull trend in Q3. BTC eventually reached $100K in December, validating the supply shock thesis once again.

Jan 2024 Macro event
Bitcoin Spot ETF
After years of rejections, the SEC approves eleven spot Bitcoin ETFs simultaneously
January 10, 2024. BlackRock's iShares, Fidelity, and nine more approved in a single day. $10B+ in week-one inflows. BlackRock's IBIT became the fastest ETF in history to $50B AUM. The institutional gateway into Bitcoin opened permanently.
After years of rejections the SEC approved 11 spot Bitcoin ETFs on January 10, 2024. BlackRock's IBIT became the fastest ETF in history to reach $10B then $50B AUM. Initial price action was a "sell the news" dip to $38K, then a rip to $73,750 all-time high by March. The approval permanently changed Bitcoin's investor base — putting it into pension funds, 401(k)s, and brokerage accounts globally. The institutional inflection point of the entire cycle.

Apr 2023 OG meme coin
$PEPE
PEPE — the meme that broke Ethereum gas and woke up a dead market
April 14, 2023. No utility, no roadmap, no presale. $1.6 billion in three weeks. The fastest meme coin to $1B in history at the time. Ethereum gas fees spiked to record levels. Thousands of wallets made life-changing returns. The first proof the market was back.
PEPE launched April 14, 2023 on Ethereum — claimed zero team allocation. It became the fastest meme coin to $1B in history. ETH gas fees hit extreme levels as wallets scrambled to buy. It single-handedly revived the meme coin category after the FTX/crypto winter. The team later controversially moved $16M from the multisig causing a crash. PEPE survived, became a permanent top-100 fixture, and directly inspired every meme coin that followed — WIF, BONK's revival, and the entire 2024 Solana meme supercycle.

May 2021 OG meme
$DOGE
Dogecoin — Elon Musk's tweets send a joke coin to $90 billion
Dogecoin had existed since 2013 as a joke. In early 2021 Elon Musk began tweeting about it relentlessly. Combined with a coordinated Reddit campaign, DOGE went from $0.003 to $0.73 — a 24,000% run — briefly entering the top-4 by market cap. The template for every celebrity-driven meme coin that followed.
A coordinated retail effort on r/SatoshiStreetBets combined with Musk's relentless Twitter activity drove DOGE from $0.003 to $0.73. At peak it had a $90B+ market cap and briefly entered the top-4. Musk appeared on Saturday Night Live to discuss it; the coin crashed during the broadcast. The event permanently established the "celebrity tweet pumps coin" playbook that every meme coin launch after it attempted to replicate.

Nov 2021 Historic rug
SQUID Game Token
The rug pull that introduced the concept to mainstream media worldwide
Launched riding the Netflix Squid Game viral moment. Went from $0.01 to $2,861 in days — with a coded mechanism that prevented selling. Developers withdrew $3.3M and disappeared. BBC, CNN, and Reuters all covered the collapse. The moment "rug pull" entered the mainstream vocabulary.
The SQUID token launched in October 2021 riding Squid Game's global cultural moment. A coded anti-dump mechanism allowed buying but not selling. It went from fractions of a cent to $2,861 as FOMO overrode all logic — even as analysts repeatedly warned about the sell restriction. Developers withdrew $3.3M from liquidity and disappeared. The token crashed to near zero in minutes. BBC, CNN, Reuters covered it. The moment "rug pull" became a phrase ordinary people understood.
